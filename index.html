what's the deal with this?


i have felt increasingly in the last few years that it is important for me to cultivate hobbies that were creative rather than consumptive. Although there is certainly significant value in travel, in hiking and in nature (all of which i really enjoy), i felt like people began to treat these as checklists or even competitions rather than as experiences and so i, personally, wanted to be conscientious in spending time creating.

i have also really enjoyed seeing my friends cultivate their own hobbies and lives, many of whom have also entered artistics pursuits like oil painting, figure drawing, music writing and reviewing. sometimes my friends will send me updates, but i want a space for people to be able to share with a group, rather than have to remember to send updates or feel self conscious about doing so.

so this is my invitation to join a "mini art collective," but in actuality it is an invitation to always share your creative works with me and your friends.



what's with the password?


that's mostly for fun and also to avoid bots scraping content. i'd like to turn this page into a full fledged internal submission thing so those with a password can view each other's updates, but that's a lofty goal and i only have so much attention. it is not a secure site by any means and if you forget the password, you can bypass it pretty easily with right click -> inspect if need be. but also you can just text me.
